Point Sparring

Point sparring is a method to demonstrate a person's techniques in a practical way by having a compiled score based from punches and kicks to the upper body.

Traditional Forms

Traditional forms focuses on the culmination of many individual techniques to demonstrate their flexibility, speed, and power through a pattern of movements.

Power Breaking

Board breaking requires the concentration of a person's breathing and focus which enables them to generate enough speed or power to break through a target.

Tenets of Taekwondo

There are five tenets of taekwondo that each practitioner strives for which are courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control, and indomitable spirit. Furthermore, these traits serve as a foundation for character development.

What is World Taekwondo (WT)?

World Taekwondo is an organization that oversees major taekwondo competitions across the globe. Furthermore, it also provides guidance and governance for Taekwondo practioners internationally which was established in 1973 in South Korea.
